Namyoung Kang is an Associate Professor in the Department of Geography at Kyungpook National University, where he leads the Climate Science Laboratory (CSL). His research focuses on climate variability and change, with an emphasis on understanding extreme weather and climate events and their societal impacts.

His work integrates observational data analysis, statistical modeling, and spatial analysis to investigate the mechanisms behind regional climate variability across East Asia and beyond. He is particularly interested in the interactions between large-scale atmospheric circulation patterns and local climate conditions.

Professor Kang's research contributes to both the fundamental understanding of the climate system and the development of practical applications for climate risk assessment and adaptation strategies.
